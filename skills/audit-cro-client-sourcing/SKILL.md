# Audit CRO Client Sourcing

Use this skill to find client-final leads for Audit CRO.

## Current priority ICP

Primary:
- US CPA / accounting / tax firms
- US law firms / attorneys
- UK solicitors
- UK accountants
- architecture / engineering / design-build firms

Secondary:
- B2B SaaS with demo/trial/pricing friction
- professional services
- high-ticket service businesses

Avoid:
- CRO agencies
- marketing agencies
- growth agencies
- web agencies
- lead-gen agencies
- funnel consultants
- software vendors selling conversion/sales/demo tools
- dropshipping
- generic Shopify micro-stores

## Sourcing method

Use micro-batches.

For US CPA/accounting:
- search by city and email pattern
- do not rely only on IRS directory
- use direct search patterns:
  - "Miami CPA firm" "info@" "contact"
  - "Tampa CPA firm" "Email:" "Contact"
  - "Florida accounting firm" "info@"
  - "CPA firm Florida" "Email:"

For law firms:
- use state bar, FindLaw, Justia, official firm websites
- verify email on official website or official public profile

## Validation rules

A lead is valid only if:
- official website loads
- public email is visible
- email source URL is provided
- company is US or UK
- client-final business confirmed
- observable CRO friction exists

Return candidates for review before writing unless the user explicitly approves writing.
